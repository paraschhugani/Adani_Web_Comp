    Faculty of Engineering Sciences and Technology

    Creating a Foundation for Tomorrow’s Nation Builders

    Technology underpins all facets of modern life and evolves at relentless speeds. Our purpose is to educate our students to participate in creating and applying contemporary knowledge and technological advances to make a difference.

    Bachelor of Technology (Civil and Infrastructure Engineering)

    The program aims to develop a strong foundation in engineering concepts and principles for efficient planning, design, and construction of related infrastructure facilities, focusing on materials, structures, transportation, water/wastewater systems, energy, etc.

    Bachelor of Technology (Information and Communication Technology)

    This program amalgams three core engineering branches: computer engineering, information technology, and electronics and communication engineering. This program aims for a unified communication system to meet modern communication requirements. It covers the storing, retrieving, manipulating, transmitting, and receiving of information which is sending and receiving of information that is vital to infrastructure development and management.

    Bachelor of Technology (Computer Science and Engineering in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)

    The 4-year full-time B.E. program provides specialization in cutting-edge technologies of Artificial Intelligence (AI) & Machine Learning (ML) with a foundation in Computer Science & Engineering. This program offers advanced expertise in a plethora of concepts related to Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Neural Networks, Data Science, Big Data Analytics, and Expert Systems. The specializations are intended to bestow assistance to students looking forward to a wide variety of careers in automated Agriculture, Health Care, Manufacturing, Education, and Public Utilities.

    Master of Technology (Construction Engineering & Management)

    The M. Tech in Construction Engineering & Management is a highly sought-after program that focuses on professional management practices applied to construction projects and portfolios. Students will learn the principles underlying construction engineering and management in construction productivity, dispute resolution, and project management. This program prepares students to lead teams while managing sustainable buildings’ design, construction, and operation. It involves an array of services during planning, design, construction, and post-construction phases to achieve project objectives such as management of quality, cost, time, and scope.

    Master of Technology (Data Science)

    The Master of Data Science is a professional degree for learners passionate about drawing meaningful knowledge from data to drive business decision-making or research output. It will develop analytical and technical skills to use data science to guide strategic decisions in your area of expertise. In this program, you will explore the latest in data mining, machine learning, and data visualization while developing the skills to effectively communicate data insights to key stakeholders.

    Master of Technology (Cyber Security & Digital Forensics)

    This program prepares professionals for the rapidly growing cybersecurity, forensics, and information assurance fields. First, you’ll unpack the core themes in Cyber Security and Forensic Information Technology, including data analysis, risk management, computer security, and cybercrime. Next, you’ll learn to apply your skills to the generation and presentation of digital evidence for use by the courts in legal or other formal proceedings. Finally, you’ll be equipped to tackle a career in forensic information technology within law firms, enforcement agencies, and IT practices.

    Master of Technology (E-Mobility)

    Renewable energy and e-mobility – these are particularly innovative, rapidly growing and future-oriented sectors in India and abroad.

    MBA (Infrastructure Management)

    This is a two-year full-time program for those who are passionate about pursuing a career in infrastructure. In the first year, students complete a core curriculum that provides a thorough, integrated grounding in management & infrastructure essentials while at the same time get exposure to a healthy dose of site visits, independent projects, and finally, end with summer internship. The idea is to make an impact on the summer recruiters on the program’s delivery which is unquestionably challenging, focused, and “real world”. In the second year, students round off their degree with umpteen number of elective courses. The electives have been designed through our continuous evaluation methodology from our parent group, alumni, and industry connects. In addition to some core courses, students round off their degree with multiple specialized electives to chose from such as : Transport, Energy, Real Estate & Urban Infrastructure.

    MBA (Energy Management)

    MBA in Energy Management is a two-year program that builds learners competencies to think logically, practically, and independently. The program gives an exclusive perspective on the future of business in the energy and power sector. Not only one will be able to enhance the skills but put into practice.

    Doctoral Programs (Full Time/ Part Time)

    Adani University established in June 2022 envisions nation building through transformative research, with thrust on infrastructure, energy, healthcare, and defense with underlying enabled by technology at all levels addressing well-being of the planet and people. At Adani University, education finds synchronization in transformation. Translation of information to implementation while transcending the boundaries of disciplines and self-conditioning, Adani University enables transformation of self, society, and the nation. Adani University offers graduate programs in multiple disciplines and encourages research at the intersection of disciplines. Engineering sciences, technology, management sciences and design find confluence in the research and other interdisciplinary initiatives impacting the society. Adani University offers doctoral research programme in the Faculty of Engineering, Sciences & Technology (FEST) and Faculty of Management Sciences (FMS) with Full & Part time engagement modes. The admissions are scheduled twice a year in July & December. Successful doctoral students shall receive an assistantship during their tenure as scholars at the University, based on their contribution to the academic. More information on the assistantship can be obtained from the “Research Fellowship” website page of the university.

    You shall be eligible for admission to the doctoral programme, if you have successfully completed one of following: 1. A 2-year / 4-semester Master’s degree programme (after 4 year undergraduate degree) with at least 60% marks in aggregate or its equivalent grade in the UGC 10- point scale (or an equivalent grade in a point scale wherever grading system is followed) or an equivalent degree from a foreign educational institution accredited by an Assessment and Accreditation Agency which is approved, recognized or authorized by an authority, established or incorporated under a law in its home country or any other statutory authority in that country to assess, accredit or assure quality and standards of educational institutions. 2. A 1-year / 2-semester Master’s degree programme, with the same conditions as in sub-clause 1 above with additional course work completion requirements. 3. A 4-year / 8-semester Bachelor’s degree with a minimum CGPA of 7.5/10 with minimum of 3 years of work experience in respective domain of specialization with the same conditions for foreign students as mentioned in sub-clause 1 with additional course work completion requirements. A relaxation of 5% marks can be given to candidate belonging to reserve category like SC/ST/OBC/Differently abled persons.

    1. The duration for Ph.D. programme shall be of maximum 3 years for full time candidate. 2. The duration Ph.D. shall be of 4 years for part time candidate including course work. However, if part time candidate supersedes all requirements before stipulated timelines, they can submit their Ph.D. thesis with prior approval from Ph.D. Supervisor and ARC. Extension beyond the above limits will be governed by Adani University Research Committee (ARC), but not more than 1 year to exceptional candidates with due consideration of reasons for the same, provided by research scholar and Ph.D. supervisor.

    Adani University admits doctoral students through two stage admission process i.e. written examination followed by research presentation. The procedure for the same shall be as follows: 1. Adani University shall admit doctoral students through a National Eligibility Test (NET) or National Entrance Test or any other relevant examination proposed by UGC, or Adani University Research Entrance Test (ARET), as per prevailing norms of the University (Refer university website for examination syllabus, pattern, and duration of examination). Those students who qualify GATE/NET/GMAT/CSIT(JRF)/other relevant examinations specified by UGC, will be exempted from Entrance Test. It is responsibility of the candidate to submit the relevant proof of the same. 2. After clearance of written examination, a research interview / presentations shall be conducted by a committee of Three experts appointed by respective head of department under Adani University. The selection shall be evaluated in the ratio 70 (written test): 30 (interview). Minimum of 50% overall marks is required to be eligible for admission to Ph.D. programme. 3. Adani University shall declare the results of entrance test and interview within 15 days after completion of research presentation. 4. Ph.D. admissions shall be done in twice a year, i.e. one admission round shall be held in winter session and second admission round shall be held in summer session. 5. Incomplete applications and applications with false information in any respect shall be summarily rejected without any intimation to the candidate.
